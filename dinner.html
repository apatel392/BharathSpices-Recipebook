Curd Rice

Step 1:
Wash the rice thrice and pressure cook with 1 & ¾ cups of water for 5 whistles in medium flame.

Step 2:
Once done, mash the rice well and mix boiled, hot milk(add butter at this stage).

Step 3:
Add more water if needed. Let it cool down.

Step 4:
Add the curd and mix well. Add all the other ingredients in the table and mix well.

Lemon Rice

Step 1:
Wash rice 2-3 times firstly. Drain water. Cook rice with water ratio 1 :2 for 3 whistles in a pressure cooker. Once done, transfer to a broad bowl and add a teaspoon of oil and a teaspoon of salt and mix carefully once and keep aside.

Step 2:
Squeeze lemon and collect the juice. Heat a kadai/ pan with oil, add mustard, once spluttered, add urad dal, chana dal, peanuts and red chilli. Fry till golden.

Step 3:
hen add green chillies, curry leaves, asafoetida and turmeric. Give a quick stir and add the lemon juice. If you want you can add a tablespoon of water and switch off the stove. Add salt and mix well.

Step 4:
Add it to the cooled rice and mix well. Adjust salt, sesame oil as desired.

Khichdi

Step 1:
In a cooker heat 1 tsp ghee and add soaked dal and rice.

Step 2:
After 2 mins, add ¼ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp salt, and 3¼ cup water. Mix well

Step 3:
After 5 mins, In a large kadai heat 2 tbsp ghee. Add 1 tsp cumin, 1 bay leaf, and pinch hing. Now add 1 onion, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste and saute well. Further, add 1 tomato and saute until the tomato turns soft and mushy.

Step 4:
Keeping the flame on low add ¼ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp garam masala, and ½ tsp salt.

Step 5:
Saute for 2 minutes or until the spices turn aromatic. Now add cooked rice and dal. Also, add 1 cup water and mix well adjusting consistency as required.

Step 6:
Finally, add 2 tbsp coriander and enjoy DAL KHICHDI with pickle and curd.
